What is this?

A continuous survey of RF spectral power data across the 300MHz-3GHz (UHF) band at/near our office in San Francisco.

View the entire spectrum at a certain point in time, or click on a point to see power in that band over time.

How did you collect this data?

We're using a BladeRF SDR and a wide-band antenna connected to a laptop to collect spectral power information using a modified version of osmocom_spectrum_sense.

Where can I get your code?

Our code is currently publicly unavailable, but we do plan on making the collection software available and providing upload API keys for anyone interested in helping us expand our survey.

Send us an email at spectrum@thisisbeep.com to get set up.
